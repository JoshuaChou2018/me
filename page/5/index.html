linux删除百万级别文件

Juexiao Zhou published on 2022-08-05 included in system
在运行任务时，往linux的同一个文件夹中写入超过500万个文件，导致在硬盘还有空间的情况下，在该文件夹下无法继续写入文件，也无法使用ls、rm等命令。记录处理过程。 Background: physical server, about two years old, 7200-RPM SATA drives connected to a 3Ware RAID card, ext3 FS mounted noatime and data=ordered, not under crazy load, kernel 2.6.18-92.1.22.el5, uptime 545 days. Directory doesn’t contain any subdirectories, just millions of small (~100 byte) files, with some larger (a few KB) ones. We have a server that has gone a bit cuckoo over the course of the last few months, but we only noticed it the other day when it started being unable to write to a directory due to it containing too many files.
